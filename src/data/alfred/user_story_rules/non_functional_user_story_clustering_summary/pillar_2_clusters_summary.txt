Pillar II – Personalized Social Inclusion – Cluster Definitions  (2 clusters available)

Cluster 2.1 Name: "Social Connectivity Features"
- Description: Requirements for features that facilitate social connection. These involve video calls, group activities, sharing events, and contact management.

Cluster 2.2 Name: "Social Privacy & Control"
- Description: Requirements ensuring users maintain control over their social presence. These include privacy settings, communication filters, and data-sharing boundaries for social interactions.

Cluster 2.3 Name: "Autonomy in Social Participation"
- Description: Requirements supporting user independence in choosing when and how to engage in social features (e.g., games, events) without pressure, and avoiding stigmatization or unwanted prompts.
