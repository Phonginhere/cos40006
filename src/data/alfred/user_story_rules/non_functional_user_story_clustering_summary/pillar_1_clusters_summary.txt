Pillar I – User-Driven Interaction Assistant – Cluster Definitions  (2 clusters available)

Cluster 1.1 Name: "Voice Interaction Quality"
- Description: Requirements related to the effectiveness of speech-based interaction. This includes speech recognition accuracy, language support, and clarity of vocal output.

Cluster 1.2 Name: "Assistant Intelligence"
- Description: Requirements addressing the system's ability to understand context and provide relevant support. These include proactive suggestions, remembering preferences, and adaptive dialogue.

Cluster 1.3 Name: "Interaction Control & Consent"
- Description: Requirements ensuring users have full autonomy in initiating, pausing, and managing interactions with ALFRED. Covers push-to-talk models, avoidance of unsolicited prompts, and explicit consent mechanisms.
