Pillar III – Effective & Personalized Care – Cluster Definitions (2 clusters available)

Cluster 3.1 Name: "Health Data Management & Security"
- Description: Requirements for handling sensitive health information. This includes permissions, audit logs, encryption, and data review options.

Cluster 3.2 Name: "Monitoring Accuracy & Reliability"
- Description: Requirements ensuring health monitoring is precise and trustworthy. These include error handling, real-time feedback, and validation mechanisms for biometric readings.

Cluster 3.3 Name: "User-Directed Care Experience"
- Description: Requirements ensuring older adults can dictate the timing, type, and intrusiveness of care interactions. Includes push-to-start reminders, refusal of check-ins, and conditional engagement with medical routines.
